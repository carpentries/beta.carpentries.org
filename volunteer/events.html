CarpentryCon

The Carpentries hosts CarpentryCon, a community-organised conference for our global community which takes place about every two years.

Dates for the next CarpentryCon have not yet been determined. If you are interested in helping to organise the next CarpentryCon, email The Carpentries Core Team.

Past CarpentryCon Events

  • CarpentryCon 2022: 30 May - 1 June, 2018 in Dublin, Ireland
  • CarpentryCon 2020: 14 July - 31 August, 2020 Online
  • CarpentryCon 2018: 1 - 12 August, 2022 Online

CarpentryConnect

CarpentryConnects are community convenings, organised to bring together community members of The Carpentries that are in close proximity geographically for knowledge exchange, collaboration, and networking.

There are currently no upcoming Carpentry Connect events.

If you are interested in hosting a CarpentryConnect in your local are the CarpentryConnect Planning Kit provides checklists, templates, recommendations for best practices and resources to serve as guides in planning and carrying out CarpentryConnect events.
