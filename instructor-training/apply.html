Instructor Training Overview

Carpentries Instructor Training is a 16-hour, hands-on training that covers the basics of educational psychology and evidence-based teaching practices. It is also the first step to become a certified Carpentries Instructor.

When Is This Form Used?

The Instructor Training Form (below) has two uses:

  • Profile Creation Form
  • Open Instructor Training Program Application

Profile Creation Form

For trainees invited to join Instructor Training by a Carpentries member organizations or groups that have arranged for training, this form is used to create your profile in our database and allows us to record attendance and track your progress through the Instructor certification process. Form responses are not reviewed in detail but may be used in programmatic assessment. In response to the first question, please select Profile Creation for Pre-approved Trainees and enter in your provided registration code.

Open Instructor Training Program Application

For individuals who wish to apply to our Open Instructor Training program, this form serves as an application. In response to the first question, please select "Open Training Application." No code is needed.

Application Criteria

  • Must be able to commit to 16-hour training, as well as pre-training reading and post-training certification activities
  • Prior teaching experience preferred
  • Experience with at least one of the technologies we teach (R, Python, OpenRefine, SQL, etc.) is recommended

What To Expect After Submitting Your Application

  1. Application Review
    Application responses are reviewed carefully, ranked, and selected to receive Instructor Training sponsored by The Carpentries. Applications are accepted on a rolling basis, meaning applications that are not accepted in the first round of review may be accepted at a later round of review based on scoring and capacity.
    Monthly
  2. Applicants Notified
    The Instructor Training Team will send out notifications to let everyone know their application status.
    3+ Months
  3. Register For A Training Event
    Accepted applicants will receive a registration code that can be used to register for an upcoming training event. After being accepted, your profile is already complete; and it is not necessary to fill out the form a second time.
    3+ Months
