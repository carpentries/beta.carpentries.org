About Instructor Training

Carpentries Instructor Training is a 16-hour, hands-on training that covers the basics of educational psychology and evidence-based teaching practices. It is also the first step to become a certified Carpentries Instructor. Instructor Training has the following goals:

  • Introduce you to evidence-based teaching practices.
  • Teach you how to create a positive environment for learners at your workshops.
  • Provide opportunities for you to practice and build your teaching skills.
  • Help you become integrated into the Carpentries community.
  • Prepare you to use these teaching skills in teaching Carpentries workshops.

Note for Open Instructor Training Applicants

If you were accepted into the Open Instructor Training program, registration will open approximately one month (~30 days) before the event start date. For all events, registration will close when the event is full or one week prior to their start date.

Attendance & Cancellation Policies

Trainees who miss more than 1 hour of an Instructor Training event may be marked absent. Instructor certification cannot be completed without full attendance at an Instructor Training event. Please be sure to verify your availability for the full instructional time of your event. If you expect to miss more than a total of 1 hour, please select a different event.

To support our program and honor the time donated by our Instructor Trainers, we ask that cancellations be made as far in advance as possible to allow other trainees to register before the 1 week deadline. If a trainee repeatedly registers and cancels (>3 times), or registers for more than one seat at the same time, they may be barred from further registration.

Trainees may cancel their registration directly within Eventbrite or via a link in the event confirmation email. After a registration has been cancelled in Eventbrite, the code that was used to register will become available to sign up for a different event.

More details on our cancellation and makeup policy are available in The Carpentries Handbook.

What To Expect After Registration

  1. Application Review
    Application responses are reviewed carefully, ranked, and selected to receive Instructor Training sponsored by The Carpentries. Applications are accepted on a rolling basis, meaning applications that are not accepted in the first round of review may be accepted at a later round of review based on scoring and capacity.
    Monthly
  2. Applicants Notified
    The Instructor Training Team will send out notifications to accepted applicants, that includes an event registration code.
    3+ Months
  3. Register For A Training Event
    Accepted applicants may use their registration code to register for an upcoming training event. After being accepted, your profile is already complete; and it is not necessary to fill out the form a second time.
    3+ Months
