Introduction

We are grateful to our supporters who help us reach the mission and vision of The Carpentries. Generous funding helps us create positive change in the data and software communities.

We are funded by member organisations, sponsors, grants, and donations. We also receive in-kind and additional support, as well as individual contributions.

We aim to be transparent in our sources of revenue and how they align with our vision and values, and funding must support existing priorities. A full report on our funding can be found here.
